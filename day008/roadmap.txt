TextSplitter in langchain

Text splitting is a process of creating small chunks of given large text, so that it's is easy to manage.
improve the quality of semantic search.
short chunks yields more accurate vectors.
prevents hallucincations and topic drift.

4 types of TextSplitter are studied.
    Length based
    Text Structure based
    Document Structure based
    Semantic meaning based

Text-Structure based splitting, here pre-defined seperators are used like 
    \n\n        paragraph
    \n          next line
    _           word splitting
    ""          split on the basis of characters.
